jhelumcardiaccenter

A cardiac centre is a type of specialty hospital where cardiovascular illnesses are diagnosed, treated, and managed. These facilities are manned by highly qualified medical specialists, such as cardiologists, heart surgeons, nurses, and support personnel, and are furnished with cutting edge medical equipment. They provide an extensive array of services, which includes heart diagnostics including cardiac catheterization, stress testing, and echocardiography. They also offer cardiac surgeries, including bypass surgery, valve replacement or repair, and heart transplants, as well as medical care of cardiac diseases and interventional cardiology procedures like angioplasty and stent implantation. Cardiac centres also place a high priority on preventive cardiology, providing services and programmes targeted at lowering heart disease risk factors via education and lifestyle changes.
