Its scope extends beyond military personnel, encompassing their families and the wider public, positioning CMH as a frontrunner in specialized medical attention. Backed by cutting-edge technology and a team of doctors, CMH Jhelum, is unwavering in its dedication to providing optimal healthcare to its valued patients.
