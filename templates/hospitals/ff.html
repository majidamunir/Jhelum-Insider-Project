Fauji Foundation provides a range of medical services, including specialist treatments across multiple disciplines, operations, tests, and consultations. In keeping with its dedication to community welfare, the hospital offers its services to civilians even though it is affiliated with the military. The hospital makes investments in cutting-edge medical technology and equipment with an emphasis on upholding high standards of care, ensuring precise diagnosis and efficient treatments. It also makes an effort to maintain its services reasonably priced, encouraging everyone to have access to healthcare. Fauji Foundation Hospital makes a substantial contribution to the general well-being of the area it serves by treating illnesses and actively pursuing preventative healthcare through community outreach programmes and health education efforts.
