Jhelum is a city on the East Bank of the Jhelum River, which is located in the district of Jhelum in the North of Punjab, Pakistan. It is the 44th largest city of Pakistan by population.

Sarai Alamgir

Sarai Alamgir is the main town of Sarai Alamgir Tehsil, located in the Gujrat district in the north of the Punjab province of Pakistan. Sarai Alamgir is one of four tehsils of Gujrat district.

Population: Approximately 93,000 people. Postal code: 49600.

Sohawa

Sohawa is a town in the Punjab province of Pakistan, and is the capital of the Sohawa Tehsil. Sohawa has grown from a small village in 1947 to a large town in 2014, with major developments in transport, education and health.

District: Jhelum
Postal code: 49230
Province: Punjab
Tehsil: Sohawa

Dina

Dina is a city in Jhelum District of Punjab the province of Pakistan. Dina is one of the oldest towns in Punjab. According to 2023 census, 50,737 people live in Dina, of which 34.12% were urban.

Postal code: 49400
Dialing code: 0544
District: Jhelum District
Division: Rawalpindi
