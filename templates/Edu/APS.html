ARMY PUBLIC SCHOOL

About

Army Public School (APS) Jhelum is a renowned educational institution that is part of the prestigious Army Public Schools and Colleges System (APSACS) in Pakistan. Established with the aim of providing quality education to the children of military personnel as well as civilians, APS Jhelum follows a rigorous academic curriculum combined with a focus on character development and extracurricular activities. The school is known for its commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development of students. It offers a wide range of facilities and resources to support the learning process, including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities.
