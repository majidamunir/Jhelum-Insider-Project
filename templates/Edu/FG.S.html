FG SCHOOL

About

FG School Jhelum is part of the Federal Government Educational Institutions (FGEIs), which are administered by the Federal Directorate of Education (FDE) in Pakistan. These schools are known for providing quality education to the children of armed forces personnel as well as civilians. FG School Jhelum follows the curriculum prescribed by the Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE), Islamabad. The school aims to provide a conducive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills among its students.
