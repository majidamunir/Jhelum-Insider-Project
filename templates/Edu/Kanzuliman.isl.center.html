KANZ-UL-IMAN JHELUM

About

Kanz-ul-Iman Quran Academy stands as a beacon of Islamic education, offering a nurturing environment for students to learn and deepen their understanding of the Quran and Islamic teachings. With a focus on providing quality education, the academy boasts experienced female tutors who are proficient in English, ensuring effective communication and learning for all students. Catering to a global audience, Kanz-ul-Iman Quran Academy welcomes students from around the world, facilitating their journey in learning the Arabic language, Quranic recitation, foundational Islamic principles, the art of prayer (Namaz), the essential Kalmas, and various supplications.
