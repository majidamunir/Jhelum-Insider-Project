MINHAJ UL QURAN JHELUM

About

Founded by Shaykh-ul-Islam Dr. Muhammad Tahir-ul-Qadri in 1981, Minhaj-ul-Qur’an International (MQI) has its headquarters in Lahore. Its mission encompasses spreading Islam's message of peace, harmony, human dignity, moderation, tolerance, and integration worldwide. Dr. Tahir-ul-Qadri's relentless efforts include delivering over 5000 lectures, authoring a thousand books, and establishing MQI centers in more than 90 countries within three decades. The vast global network of MQI centers necessitates efficient coordination, which is facilitated by the Directorate of Foreign Affairs (DFA) at the MQI Headquarters in Lahore. The DFA works towards various objectives, including Da’wa and propagation of true Islamic teachings, moral and spiritual reformation, revival of Islamic sciences, and promotion of peace, tolerance, and interfaith harmony.
