FAIZAN-E-MADINAH

About

Faizan-e-Madinah, associated with the renowned organization Dawat-e-Islami, is a vibrant center in Jhelum dedicated to spreading the teachings of Islam and promoting a message of peace, tolerance, and spirituality. Dawat-e-Islami, founded by Maulana Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri, is a global Islamic movement that strives to educate people about the true essence of Islam and its principles. Faizan-e-Madinah in Jhelum serves as a hub for various religious and educational activities. The center offers a range of programs and services, including religious lectures, Quranic teachings, and community outreach initiatives. It acts as a platform for individuals to enhance their understanding of Islam and strengthen their connection with Allah.
