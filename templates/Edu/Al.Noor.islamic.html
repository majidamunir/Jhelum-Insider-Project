AL NOOR INTERNATIONAL

About

The Al-Noor Welfare Foundation, initiated by Scholar Nighat Hashmi in 1996, serves as a beacon of spiritual enlightenment and educational empowerment. Rooted in the essence of Islamic teachings, the foundation embodies the sacred mission of nurturing souls and fostering a deep connection with the divine. It emerged as a response to the pressing need for character refinement among the Muslim youth, echoing the timeless wisdom of the Quran and Sunnah in guiding hearts towards righteousness and virtue. With a steadfast commitment to spiritual growth and moral upliftment, Al-Noor International radiates the light of knowledge and spirituality, illuminating paths towards a more enlightened and harmonious society..
