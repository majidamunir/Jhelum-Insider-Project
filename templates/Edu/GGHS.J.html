GOVT. GIRLS HIGH SCHOOL JADA JHELUM

About

Govt Girls High School (GGHS) Jada, situated in the vibrant Jhelum District of Punjab, Pakistan, is a cornerstone of educational advancement in the region. With a sizable student body of 834 and a dedicated faculty of 23 teachers, GGHS Jada operates within a framework of excellence.Under the leadership of Headmistress Shahnaz Akhter, the school strives to overcome these obstacles and provide quality education to its students.
