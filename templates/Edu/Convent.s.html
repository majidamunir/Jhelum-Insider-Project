About

Convent High School Jhelum is known for its dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals. The school offers a wide range of academic subjects and extracurricular activities to cater to the diverse interests and talents of its students. With a rich history and tradition of excellence, Convent High School Jhelum continues to uphold its reputation as a center of learning and academic achievement in the region. Through its comprehensive educational programs and emphasis on moral and ethical values, the school aims to prepare students for success in their academic pursuits and beyond.
