GOVT. GIRLS HIGH NO.1 JHELUM

About

Government Girls High School No.1 (GGHS No.1) Jhelum, situated in the Jhelum District of Punjab Province, Pakistan, stands as a beacon of education in the region. Boasting a student body of 1801 individuals and a dedicated faculty consisting of 56 teachers, GGHS No.1 is committed to providing quality education to its students.Under the leadership of Headmistress Safia Cheema, GGHS No.1 continues to prioritize academic excellence and character development. For those interested in learning more about admissions, basic facilities, sports amenities, academic resources, student enrollment across different classes, and staff profiles detailing their designations and qualifications, the school welcomes inquiries and encourages visits to its premises.
