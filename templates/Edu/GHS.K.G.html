GOVT. HIGH SCHOOL KALA GUJRAN

About

GHS Kala Gujran in Jhelum is a pivotal educational institution serving the local community. Located in the heart of Kala Gujran, the school is committed to providing quality education to its students. With a curriculum aligned with educational standards, the school offers a diverse range of subjects including mathematics, science, social studies, and languages. The dedicated faculty members ensure a conducive learning environment, while the school's facilities, such as modern classrooms, laboratories, and sports facilities, support the holistic development of students. GHS Kala Gujran also emphasizes extracurricular activities, fostering talents and interests beyond academics.
