JAMIA-ASRIA JHELUM

About

Jamia Asria Jhelum stands as a testament to the rich heritage of Islamic education in the region. Founded with a vision to propagate the teachings of Islam and instill a deep understanding of the Quran and Sunnah, Jamia Asria has been a guiding light for seekers of knowledge and spirituality. Nestled amidst the serene surroundings of Jhelum, this esteemed institution offers a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses both traditional Islamic sciences and modern disciplines. With a dedicated faculty of scholars and educators, Jamia Asria endeavors to cultivate not only academic excellence but also moral integrity and spiritual enlightenment among its students. Through its holistic approach to education, the institution aims to produce well-rounded individuals who are equipped to contribute positively to society while upholding the timeless values of Islam.
